The Fantastic Four: First Steps (or The Fantastic 4: First Steps) is a 2025 American superhero film directed by Matt Shakman from a screenplay written by Jeff Kaplan, Ian Springer, Josh Friedman, and Eric Pearson, based on the Fantastic Four superhero team from Marvel Comics. Produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, the film is the 37th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and serves as the second reboot of the Fantastic Four film series.


The film features an ensemble cast consisting of Pedro Pascal, Vanessa Kirby, Joseph Quinn, and Ebon Moss-Bachrach as the main characters, alongside Ralph Ineson, Julia Garner, Paul Walter Hauser, John Malkovich, Natasha Lyonne, and Sarah Niles in supporting roles.


In the film, the Fantastic Four must protect Earth, a retro-futuristic 1960s world, which is threatened to be consumed by the cosmic planet-eating entity Galactus. The movie explores themes of family, heroism, and sacrifice while delivering spectacular visual effects and action sequences that showcase the unique powers of each team member.
